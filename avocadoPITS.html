avocado pits

PROPERTIES

  • Starch can be extracted from the pits.

  • Storage and Processing.

  • Dry in sun, a dehydrator or in oven at the lowest temperature setting. If your oven has a fan, turn it on.

  • Grind in food processor, blender, or coffee grinder.

  • Use a sieve to separate different granularities. (optional)

  • Store in a seeled container.
